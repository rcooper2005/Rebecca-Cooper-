About Dr. Cooper

Bridging Science, Education & Technology

Dr. Cooper is a professor with backgrounds in biology, environmental science, and science education. Her leadership includes eight years as Chair of Assessment and Graduate Department Chair, and she currently serves as the Director of Assessment at Georgia Gwinnett College.

With wide-ranging experience teaching science content, methods, and general education courses to undergraduate and graduate students, Dr. Cooper has notably developed and taught online courses for over 18 years. Beyond instruction and supervision of field students, she provides support and training to faculty on integrating technology and online instruction.

Her research on pre-service teachers' perceptions of AI has gained international recognition, most recently through her presentation at the eLearn 2025 World Conference on EdTech, where she shared findings on pre-service educators' evolving perspectives on AI for professional development and learning.
